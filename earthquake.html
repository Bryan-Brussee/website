    Earthquake by magnitude

    The above map was built with Leaflet and D3 and based off of a tutorial developed by Malcolm Mclean. Data was gathered from USGS earthquake database.

    The size of each circle corresponds to that earthquake's magnitude. Logarithmic scaling was employed for general readability.
