    Life expectancy by income per capita

    Built with JavaScript's D3 library and data from GapMinder, this chart plots average life expectancy as a function of income per capita. Logarithmic scaling is employed on the X axis to save space and increase general readability.

    When viewed in this fashion, the positive correlation between these two variables becomes clear.
